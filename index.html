Nourishment & Hydration

  • Hydration: Drink plenty of water throughout the day, especially if breastfeeding.
  • Listen to Your Body: Eat nutritious foods that make you feel energized. Your needs may be higher while you are active and recovering.

Listen to Your Body

  • Rest is Productive: Don't be afraid to take extra rest days. Recovery is crucial for progress.
  • No Pain, No Pain: You should not feel pain during or after your runs. If you do, stop and consult a professional.
